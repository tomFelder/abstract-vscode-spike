## Purpose

This policy defines how access to information systems is granted, reviewed, and revoked so that
only authorised people can reach the data and systems they need.

## Authentication

All user accounts authenticate against the central identity provider. Passwords must meet the
organisation's complexity standard and are never shared between users.

Administrative access to production systems is granted on a least-privilege basis and reviewed
every quarter.

## Account lifecycle

Accounts are provisioned on joining, adjusted on role change, and disabled on the same day a
person leaves. Dormant accounts are disabled after 60 days of inactivity.
