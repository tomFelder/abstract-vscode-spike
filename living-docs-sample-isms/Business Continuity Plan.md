---
title: Business Continuity Plan
subtitle: ISMS plan - reviewed annually
---

## Purpose

This plan ensures the organisation can continue or quickly resume critical operations after a
disruption.

## Critical services

Critical services and their recovery-time objectives are identified and reviewed each year.

## Backups

Data is backed up on a defined schedule and restores are tested periodically.

## Invocation

The plan is invoked by the continuity lead. Staff are notified through the agreed communication
channels.
