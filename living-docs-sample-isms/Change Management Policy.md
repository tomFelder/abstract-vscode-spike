## Purpose

This policy defines how changes to production systems are proposed, approved, and recorded.

## Standard changes

Changes are raised with a description, a risk assessment, and a rollback plan, then reviewed and
approved before deployment.

## Emergency changes

Emergency changes may be deployed to resolve an urgent issue. The reason and outcome are recorded
after the fact.

## Records

All changes are logged so that the state of production systems can be reconstructed.
