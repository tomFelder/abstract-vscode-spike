## Purpose

This policy defines what events are logged, how logs are protected, and how long they are kept.

## What is logged

Authentication events, administrative actions, and access to sensitive data are logged centrally
to the security information and event management (SIEM) platform.

## Retention

Security logs are retained for 6 months. Logs are protected against tampering and access is
restricted to the security team.

## Monitoring

Alerts are reviewed daily. Suspected incidents are escalated under the Incident Response Plan.
