---
title: Backup Policy
subtitle: ISMS policy - reviewed annually
---

## Purpose

This policy defines how organisational data is backed up so it can be recovered after loss or
corruption.

## Schedule

Critical data is backed up daily and less critical data weekly. Backups are stored separately
from the primary systems.

## Testing

Restores are tested periodically to confirm backups are usable.

## Retention

Backups are retained according to the record-specific schedule and then securely destroyed.
