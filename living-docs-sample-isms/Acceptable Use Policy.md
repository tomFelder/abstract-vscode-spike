## Purpose

This policy sets out how staff may use organisational systems, devices, and data.

## Acceptable use

Systems are provided for work purposes. Limited personal use is permitted where it does not
interfere with work or breach any policy.

## Personal devices

Personal devices used for work must be enrolled in mobile device management before accessing any
organisational data.

## Prohibited use

Users must not install unapproved software, disable security controls, or share credentials.
