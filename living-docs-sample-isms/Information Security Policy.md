## Purpose

This is the organisation's top-level information security policy. It sets the direction for the
information security management system (ISMS) and the supporting policies.

## Scope

The ISMS covers all organisational information, the systems that process it, and the people who
handle it.

## Objectives

The organisation protects the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information in line
with its risk appetite and legal obligations.

## Responsibilities

Management owns the ISMS. All staff are responsible for following the supporting policies.
