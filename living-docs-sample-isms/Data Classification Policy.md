## Purpose

This policy defines the classification levels used to label organisational information and the
handling rules for each.

## Classification levels

Information is classified as Public, Internal, or Confidential. Confidential is the most
restricted level and covers customer data and security records.

## Handling

Confidential information is encrypted in transit and at rest and is shared only on a need-to-know
basis. Retention follows the record-specific schedule.

## Labelling

Documents and datasets carry their classification so that handling rules are clear to everyone.
