## Purpose

This policy governs how third-party suppliers that handle organisational data are assessed and
managed.

## Assessment

Suppliers are risk-assessed before engagement. The depth of assessment is proportionate to the
sensitivity of the data they handle.

## Contracts

Contracts with suppliers include confidentiality, data-protection, and security obligations, and
the right to audit where the risk warrants it.

## Ongoing management

Supplier performance and security posture are monitored through the life of the engagement.
