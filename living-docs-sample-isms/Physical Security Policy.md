title: Physical Security Policy
subtitle: ISMS policy - reviewed annually
---

## Purpose

This policy protects the organisation's premises, equipment, and the information they hold.

## Access to premises

Entry to work areas is controlled by badge access. Visitors are signed in and escorted.

## Equipment

Equipment holding organisational data is secured and disposed of securely at end of life.

## Clear desk

Sensitive materials are locked away when unattended and screens are locked when workstations are
left.
