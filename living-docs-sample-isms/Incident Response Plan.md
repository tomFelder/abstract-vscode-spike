## Purpose

This plan sets out how the organisation detects, responds to, and recovers from security
incidents.

## Roles

The response lead coordinates the response. Team members are assigned to containment,
communications, and recovery.

## Reporting

Suspected incidents are reported to the response lead as soon as they are identified. Confirmed
incidents are triaged within 72 hours.

## Recovery and review

After containment, systems are restored from known-good backups and a post-incident review
captures lessons learned.
