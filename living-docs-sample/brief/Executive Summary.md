# Executive Summary

The Northwind rebrand modernises the brand without losing its heritage. This summary
is for the leadership team; the full detail lives in the Project Brief.

## Direction

We are standardising on a confident, calm visual language. The primary colour is blue,
used sparingly so it always signals the most important action. Secondary surfaces stay
neutral so the blue carries weight wherever it appears.

## Outcome

By keeping the primary colour consistent across the site, the product, and email,
customers get one recognisable Northwind wherever they meet us.
