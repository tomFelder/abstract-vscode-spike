# Project Brief — Northwind Rebrand

## Overview

This brief sets the direction for the Northwind rebrand. It is the canonical source
for the visual language; the Executive Summary and the Appendix both follow the
decisions recorded here.

## Visual identity

The primary colour is blue (#1E5BFF). It anchors the logo, primary buttons, and
links across every surface. Headlines pair the primary colour with a neutral ink,
and the blue is reserved for the single most important action on a screen.

## Scope

The rebrand covers the marketing site, the product shell, and the email templates.
Each surface adopts the primary colour for its principal call to action.
